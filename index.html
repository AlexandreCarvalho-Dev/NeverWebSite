Bio

Never is more than an artist — he’s a persona born from survival.
Created by André Teixeira, a young artist from Portugal, Never became the voice and shield for a kid growing up in chaos. Where André carried scars, Never carried fire. What began as an alter ego quickly evolved into identity: the armor, the attitude, the sound, the life.

In 2021, Never released his first project Get Away, a melodic, atmospheric trap EP that channeled escapism during the suffocating COVID years. It was his first attempt at breaking free — laced with dreamy production and a hunger for adventure.

He leveled up with After All, a moody and soulful project that played like pages ripped from a diary, capturing the loss of innocence. Heartbreak, self-doubt, and overthinking became verses, and the track Too Much caught fire, landing him on Spotify’s editorial playlist Seda.

For the first time, Never’s pain reached thousands of ears — and stayed there. Now, Never is building something bigger: a universe. His upcoming project Echos is designed to be more than an album — it’s the next chapter of his story, touching on trust issues, substances, and the darker corners of self-reflection. For Never, music isn’t just songs. It’s therapy, identity, and legacy. André may have been broken, but Never is inevitable. Even the name is a vow — a promise to “Never Fade Away.”
